And drag this link: Subscribe to hCalendar to your links toolbar and one-click subscribe to hCalendar events from webpages to your calendar program.

The H2VX events service converts web pages with hCalendar microformats (What are microformats?) for events to iCalendar (ics format) for convenient import into your calendar program. See the hCalendar specification for more information.
